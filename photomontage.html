Photomontage

The concept I’m exploring for my photomontage is “Duality.” Duality is a concept that appears alot in the world around us and it’s something that not many people talk about often unless the situation were to call for it outside of more commonly known aspects of it. Life and death, love and hate, man and woman and the like but also our minds and the world and the world vs. the universe itself. When talking about things like films or books, often I’ll find that’s it’s those who have more in-depth knowledge of the story that will or point out hidden duality found through the chapters or episodes or films or a series. The small things between two characters that you may not pick up on until it becomes a larger part of the story and plot and makes them stand out from each other.

To me, when it comes to the concept of duality it feels like the existence is like a building carefully constructed out of multiple dualities; and those combined dualities give a sense of balance to things sometimes. That’s what I hope to convey with my montage. Imagines for my concept we tricky for me as I couldn’t find more that I felt fit and some of the ones I did find weren’t in the public domain for free use. The ones I did use however came from places like: Upsplash (the apartment building) Shutterstock (the sun and moon), Public Domain Image Archive (the live woman and dead skeleton), and Adobe Stock (Yin and Yang).
