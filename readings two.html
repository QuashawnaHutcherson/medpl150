AI Slop Reading Response

Going based on my own personal experience involving gen AI, I agree with article written by Ms./Mrs. Malik. As time goes on it gets harder to avoid seeing brain rot filled images posted on social media using gen AI and I have yet to see anything positive come out of it. Especially whenever I see political stuff on my feeds created by accounts with blue check marks. It’s reached a point where I have to look into what I see because some of it is getting hard to distinguish from real images. In addition to this, I know a few artists online who have had their work stolen and fed into AI sites and posted by the person who did it as if it were their own. I hear about situations like this more each day, and I can’t help but worry about what if we start seeing A.I political ads or hear messages using a gen A.I voice on TV’s or radios. I’ve reached a point where I don’t want to post any photos online out of fear someone might take them and make something creepy with it using AI. So far, since the tool has taken off, I feel less safe with it and more paranoid of it. Sure, some people really do use it for silly antics and mean no harm, but the lack of regulations regarding AI content and the constant sightings of it being used in such negative matters has left me with a bad feeling about it. No, I don’t feel this way because of the tool itself, but because of what people are already willing to do with it. I frankly feel as though it’s evolving way too fast for any of our own good. Of course, there are forms of AI that have been around for a while though, and I think are helpful. The ones often used to help in animations and movies, compared to their generative counterpart seem much more useful. I also feel like the AI used in the medical field is helpful as well, I’ve seen that these forms of AI are being used to help find ways to treat certain illnesses and I strongly think that A.I should be used for things like these more so than with the general public, at least for right now. I do believe there could be a day where Gen AI is actually far more helpful, and then people will eventually mellow out and get use to AI and what it can do. But right now, it feels like so many people are over excited about it and it’s has reached levels of carelessness that can cause harm to someone’s life. Maybe AI can be helpful in the education field when it comes to the arts as well but only if it’s used wisely and with permission and guidelines. Overall, for the time being, to call the recent creations of gen AI “AI Slop” feels very accurate to me but I hope that eventually changes with time.
